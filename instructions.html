Welcome to the map generator!

Instructions on how to use the map generator:

The menu:

Width and height inputs affect the map width and height, only enter in integers greater than zero. The information image when clicked on will pull up this menu if you need a refresher on how to use the page. Download image button will save the map as an image to your computer. Terrain buttons are currently not working and are being worked on.

The navigation compass:

Click on the directional arrows to move around the map or use the arrow keys on your keyboard.

The zoom buttons:

Click on the "+" or "-" in the bottom right corner to zoom in and out of the map.
